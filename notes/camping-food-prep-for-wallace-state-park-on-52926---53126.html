Camping Prep & Menu Summary: Mace Family Trip

The Weekend Menu Schedule

Day Breakfast Lunch Dinner
Friday — — Pulled Pork Tacos & Quesadillas
Saturday Egg & Sausage Burritos Pulled Pork & Hummus Wraps Taco-Beef Hobo Packets
Sunday Mountain House + Toppings (Departure) —

4-Day Prep Schedule

  • Monday (Holiday):
    • Prepare pulled pork (if not done). Divide into Friday/Saturday portions.
    • Peel/cube sweet potatoes and par-boil for 5 minutes. Drain and dry completely.
  • Tuesday (Evening):
    • Mix ground beef with taco seasoning.
    • Assemble "Hobo" packets: Beef, par-boiled sweet potatoes, and a spoonful of salsa. Wrap in parchment paper then heavy-duty foil.
    • Note: Mark the 5-year-old’s packet (meat/potato only) with a Sharpie.
  • Wednesday (Evening):
    • Scramble eggs and sausage.
    • Crucial: Cool mixture completely before rolling into burritos.
    • Wrap individually in foil and store in a sealed bag.
  • Thursday (Evening):
    • Wash and portion fruit (grapes, berries).
    • Slice apples (toss with lemon juice) and green onions.
    • Pack the "Kitchen Bin" (Mountain House, spices, oil, foil, trash bags).
    • Assemble the "Friday-First" cooler layout.

Final Shopping List

Proteins & Dairy

  • Pulled Pork (Pre-cooked)
  • Lean Ground Beef (1.5 lbs)
  • Breakfast Sausage (1 lb)
  • Eggs (1.5 dozen)
  • Shredded Cheddar/Mexican Blend Cheese
  • Greek Yogurt
  • String Cheese

Produce

  • Sweet Potatoes (3–4)
  • Green Onions (1 bunch)
  • Grapes, Berries, Bananas
  • Apples (2–3)

Pantry

  • Mountain House Pouches (Backup)
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Whole Wheat Tortillas (Large pack)
  • Individual Hummus Cups
  • Salsa
  • Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder

Supplies

  • Heavy-duty Aluminum Foil
  • Parchment Paper
  • Trash bags

Camping Cooking Guide

  • Friday Dinner: Grill-toast quesadillas (kids) and warm pulled pork (adults) on grill grate. Top with salsa, cheese, and green onions.
  • Saturday Breakfast: Heat foil-wrapped burritos on grill grate (10–12 mins, flip once).
  • Saturday Lunch: Spread hummus on tortilla, add cold pulled pork, and roll. Serve with fruit/cheese.
  • Saturday Dinner: Place beef/potato packets on hot coals or grill grate (20–25 mins).
  • Sunday Breakfast: Boil water in Jetboil. Prepare Mountain House pouches and stir in shredded cheese, green onions, and salsa.
